Sublime Text 2 Pro Tip: Multiple Cursors

Sep 3rd, 2013

This Sublime Text 2 feature is a real-time saver: 1. Place your cursor within the word and press Ctrl+Cmd+G. 2. This selects every occurrence of that word and creates multiple cursors. 3. Edit the word and watch the multiple cursors do the same.

Learn about Multiple Cursors and other pro tips from the article 10 Must-know Sublime Text 2 tips.
